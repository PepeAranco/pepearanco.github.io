Design Challenge 1

Which two websites are you most inspired by?

I was most inspired by the home page of TalkPR and by the home page of Atelier Desbains.

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

TalkPR uses a very clear focal point, a big, colorful and dynamic logo, centered on a white background. Hierarchy is clear, the text under the logo describes the company in a few words, giving the user the basic information about the brand in big black letters. The menu bar at the bottom of the page contributes in my opinion to the focal point and hierarchy by centering the users attention on the first two objects. User will naturally scroll down to see more, this action prompts the menu bar to move to the top of the page, where most users are used to se it.For the following content I like the choice of a 4 column grid, combining colorful images and squares with a little text, presenting them in different shapes and sizes but keeping certain structure.

Atelier Desbains caught my attention because of the use of a very dark image as white space, creates a huge contrast with the rest of the page. As we scroll down, big white backgrounds with small centered and gray text, and 3 column grids with bold, colorful images make this a very easy and pleasant website to look at, user should easily find what they are looking for.

What makes them similar and different

Both sites have a strong presence as soon as you land on the home page and develop their information to the user as we scroll down the page. The main difference I found is on the use of white space. Atelier Desbains uses a lot of white space as we scroll down the page, simple and organized content in a 3 column format. Talk PR uses almost the entire with of the site with the table, the white spaces are sort of incorporated in the columns given the visual effect of the text boxes mixed in with the images.
